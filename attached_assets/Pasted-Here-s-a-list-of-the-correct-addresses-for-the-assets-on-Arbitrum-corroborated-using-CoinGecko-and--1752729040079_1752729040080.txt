Regarding "Aave Asset Balance Errors":

The error message "Error - Could not transact with/call contract function, is contract deployed correctly and chain synced?" indicates a deeper issue than just an incorrect address. This error typically arises when:

Incorrect Network: You might be trying to interact with a contract on a different network than where it's deployed (e.g., trying to call an Arbitrum contract while your wallet is on Ethereum Mainnet). Ensure your wallet is connected to Arbitrum One.

Node/RPC Issues: The RPC (Remote Procedure Call) endpoint you're using to connect to Arbitrum might be having issues, or your connection is unstable.

Contract Not Deployed (or different version): While less likely for major assets like aWBTC and aWETH on Aave, it's possible you're interacting with a very old or incorrect contract address if it's not the exact, current Aave V3 address.

Chain Syncing Issues: Your local node or the public RPC you're using might not be fully synced with the Arbitrum blockchain, leading to an inability to interact with contracts.
