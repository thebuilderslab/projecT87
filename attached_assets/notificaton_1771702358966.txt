The Notification Architecture
1. The Publisher (The DeFi Engine)
Whenever the bot successfully completes a major action in strategy_engine.py (e.g., a Capacity Borrow, a Macro Short, or a Nurse Sweep), it will call a new database function: db.add_notification(user_wallet, message).

2. The Subscribers (The UIs and Agents)

The Human (Flask Dashboard): We will add a simple "Inbox" or "Activity Feed" page to the Flask dashboard that queries this table so the user can see a ledger of everything your platform has done for them.

The AI (FastAPI): We will create a new endpoint (GET /api/v1/notifications) so the OpenClaw agent can programmatically check its own inbox and act on the information (e.g., "Oh, the Macro Short activated, I should pause trading").
