Most yield products ask: “how much return can we extract?” Project 87 asks: “how do we generate sustainable USDC income at the lowest possible risk, indefinitely?” The Deleverage Shield is the mechanism that makes that permanent — it ensures the position can never compound risk. That’s unusual in DeFi, where most strategies silently accumulate risk as they generate yield.
// SYSTEM CAPABILITIES
◈
Autonomous Yield Engine
Connects to a lending protocol and DEX to run continuous borrow-swap-accumulate cycles. Your collateral works around the clock — no manual trades, no gas calculations.
⬡
Deleverage Shield
On a recurring schedule, the engine pulls USDC from your treasury and repays open debt. Position risk compresses over time rather than accumulating — a built-in risk floor.
⟐
Nurse Mode Safety
Continuously monitors collateral velocity. If markets drop sharply, non-profit assets are swept into the lending pool as additional collateral before liquidation thresholds are reached.
◇
Non-Custodial Architecture
Your collateral stays in your wallet at all times. Credit delegation allows the engine to borrow and repay on your behalf without ever taking possession of your assets.
▲
Growth Probability Engine
ML-driven scoring calculates execution probability each cycle. The engine switches between Growth, Capacity, and Emergency paths dynamically based on live market conditions.
◉
USDC Treasury
Profit accumulates in pure USDC — stable, claimable on demand, and permanently protected from safety sweep mechanisms. Track milestones live on your field operations dashboard.

// WHO IT'S FOR
01
Long-term holders who need liquidity without selling
You believe in ETH long-term but need dollars today. Selling means a taxable event and losing your position. Project 87 generates USDC income against your ETH without selling it — you keep the upside, you get the cash flow.
02
Crypto-native retirement / passive income
For someone replacing a traditional savings account or bond ladder with crypto. Project 87 runs continuously whether you check it or not, generating a predictable stream of USDC that can be withdrawn on demand via the API — essentially a programmable yield account.
03
DAO operating expense management
DAOs typically hold large ETH/stablecoin treasuries that sit idle. Project 87 generates a USDC stream from the ETH portion of the treasury to fund ongoing operational costs, without the DAO having to vote on asset sales every time it needs to pay contributors.
04
Pre-liquidity founders and vested token holders
Founders or early employees with vested crypto they can't or don't want to sell yet. Project 87 generates cash flow against locked or illiquid positions without triggering a sale. The milestone countdowns give them a forward-looking view of treasury growth.
05
Real estate investors bridging between deals
Someone with capital parked between property closings. Instead of sitting in a savings account at 4%, the idle capital is put to work generating USDC, with the full principal accessible whenever the next deal closes.
06
Crypto allocators who want yield with a defined risk ceiling
Unlike leveraged yield strategies where risk grows with the position, Project 87's Health Factor floor means the maximum risk is bounded by design. The Deleverage Shield is a built-in stop-loss that reduces debt instead of closing the position — suitable for allocators who need to comply with risk mandates.
