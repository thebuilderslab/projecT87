Project 87 is an autonomous liquidity engine that borrows against your ETH and Bitcoin collateral, converts proceeds into USDC, and repays debt automatically — generating a self-growing treasury with no daily management required.

// SYSTEM CAPABILITIES
◈
Autonomous Yield Engine
Connects to Aave V3 and Uniswap V3 to run borrow-swap-accumulate cycles every 2 minutes. Your collateral works around the clock — no manual trades, no gas calculations.
⬡
Deleverage Shield
Every 18 minutes, the engine pulls USDC from your treasury and repays open debt. Position risk compresses over time rather than accumulating — a built-in risk floor.
⟐
Nurse Mode Safety
Monitors collateral velocity every 5 minutes. If markets drop sharply, non-profit assets are swept into Aave as additional collateral before liquidation thresholds are reached.
◇
Non-Custodial Architecture
Your collateral stays in your wallet at all times. Credit delegation allows the engine to borrow and repay on your behalf without ever taking possession of your assets.
▲
Growth Probability Engine
ML-driven scoring calculates execution probability each cycle. The engine switches between Growth, Capacity, and Emergency paths dynamically based on live market conditions.
◉
USDC Treasury
Profit accumulates in pure USDC — stable, claimable on demand, and permanently protected from safety sweep mechanisms. Track milestones live on your field operations dashboard.
